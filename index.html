Real Estate Professional Status (REPS)
REPS applies per person and requires both: more than 750 hours in real property trades/businesses you materially participate in, and more than half of all your working time in real property trades/businesses. Pick a person above to evaluate their status.
Short-Term Rental Material Participation
A rental averaging ≤7 days per guest isn't a "rental activity" under §469. If the person materially participates (commonly 500 hrs, or 100 hrs and more than anyone else), losses can be non-passive.
